These terms help learners talk about what they are seeing, building, and improving.

## Core Terms

### Engineering Tradeoff
A choice where improving one part of a system can make another part harder to manage. In this project, more speed can reduce control or efficiency.

### Iteration
Making a version, testing it, learning from the result, and improving it.

### Efficiency
How well a car uses its available energy or fuel while still performing the job.

### Handling
How controllable the car feels when turning, avoiding obstacles, or reacting at speed.

### Sustainability
Making design decisions with resource use and long-term impact in mind.

### Telemetry
Performance information collected during a run, such as speed, timing, or collisions.

### Remix
Changing a project on purpose to test an idea, personalize it, or improve how it works.

### Skillmap
A guided MakeCode Arcade pathway that unlocks activities step by step.

## Teaching Tip

Define terms only when learners can connect them to something they just saw happen in the game. The word lands better when the example is fresh.

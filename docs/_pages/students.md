## Welcome to Driven by STEM! 🏎️

Ready to race? This isn't just about speed—it's about smart engineering choices that make you faster, safer, and more efficient on the track.

In this game, you'll learn how real F1 engineers make decisions by testing different tradeoffs in your own race car.

## What You'll Do

Jump into a fast-paced racing experience where you can:

- 🏎️ Drive and test your car on the track
- ⚙️ Tune your car's speed, handling, and efficiency
- 🔬 See how each change affects your performance
- 🎯 Make smart tradeoffs between speed and control
- 💻 Change the code and watch what happens
- 🏆 Compare different strategies and find what works best

## What You'll Learn

Through gameplay, you'll discover:

- How code changes create real results you can see and feel
- Why engineers balance performance, safety, and sustainability
- How to test ideas, learn from results, and improve your design
- What careers in racing, software, and engineering look like
- That the best solution isn't always the fastest one

## How to Get Started

**Follow the skillmap activities:**

- Each activity teaches you one new skill
- You'll build the game step by step using code blocks
- Test your changes in the simulator right away
- Complete each challenge to unlock the next one

## Tips for Success

- Don't worry about getting it perfect—testing and trying again is how engineers work!
- Pay attention to how different values change the way your car handles
- Think about real race cars: what matters more on different tracks?
- Try extreme values to see what happens, then find the sweet spot
- Share your discoveries with classmates and compare strategies

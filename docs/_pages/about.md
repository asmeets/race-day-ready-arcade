## The Partnership

**Driven by STEM** is a collaborative educational initiative bringing together three world-leading organizations:

### 🏎️ Formula 1
The pinnacle of motorsport engineering, where technology, strategy, and human performance converge at speeds over 200 mph.

### 🏁 Mercedes-AMG Petronas Formula One Team
Eight-time Constructors' World Champions, pioneering sustainable high-performance racing and inspiring the next generation of engineers.

### 💻 Microsoft
Global technology leader committed to empowering every person and organization through accessible tools like MakeCode that make computer science education engaging and inclusive.

## The Mission

To inspire middle school students to explore STEM careers by experiencing real engineering tradeoffs through hands-on coding and gameplay.

Through the **Driven by STEM** MakeCode Arcade project, students discover that engineering isn't just about making things faster—it's about making smart decisions that balance performance, efficiency, safety, and sustainability.

## Why Racing?

Racing provides an immediate, visceral way to understand engineering principles:

- **Visible tradeoffs**: More speed vs. better control
- **Measurable outcomes**: Lap times, fuel efficiency, collision impact
- **Real-world relevance**: Connection to careers in automotive, software, data analysis, and sustainability
- **Engaging gameplay**: Learning through play, not lectures

## Educational Approach

This project is designed with educators and learners in mind:

- ✅ **Classroom-ready**: No complex setup, works in browsers
- ✅ **Remixable**: Students can modify and experiment with code
- ✅ **Accessible**: Simple controls, clear feedback, inclusive design
- ✅ **Standards-aligned**: Supports CSTA, NGSS, and ISTE standards
- ✅ **Career-connected**: Links gameplay to real STEM professions

## The Technology

Built with **Microsoft MakeCode Arcade**, a block-based and text-based programming platform that:

- Runs entirely in the browser—no installation needed
- Supports visual blocks for beginners and JavaScript/TypeScript for advanced learners
- Provides immediate feedback through a built-in game simulator
- Enables easy sharing and remixing of projects

Use this section for the high-level timing and pacing of a standard session.

## Recommended 45-60 Minute Agenda

### 1. Welcome and Framing

- introduce the challenge and define tradeoff in plain language
- explain how teams will build, test, and compare

### 2. Guided Build

- launch the skillmap and let learners work through the first core steps
- pause once a visible mechanic change has happened

### 3. Compare Results

- ask teams what changed and what effect they noticed
- surface one or two different strategies from the room

### 4. Remix or Replay

- give teams time to revise one choice or test a second setup
- keep the loop short so the whole room can finish together

### 5. Reflection and Closing

- use one prompt from the Feedback section
- connect learner decisions to roles and engineering thinking

## Shorter Event Version

For a fast activation, trim the build time, use one compare-and-explain pause, and close with a single spoken reflection instead of a written artifact.
